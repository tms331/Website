  Python Projects - https://github.com/tms331/Python-Projects
 🞄 This project was to create a basic AI, a "bot" that will reach a particular cell with a "button" that will put out a "fire". The project essentially was to play a game in which a fire and button are placed in random cells of a large "square" or 2d array. We were tasked with making different strategies for our bot that would best allow it to succeed in putting out a fire that would spread via reaching the button before the fire did or before the bot's death.
